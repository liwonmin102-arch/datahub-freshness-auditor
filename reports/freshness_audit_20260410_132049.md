Immediate intervention is required to restart staging processing and investigate data loss.

### 2. [CRITICAL] stage_mismatch — `staging_trips`

### 3. [CRITICAL] staleness — `mart_daily_summary`

**Evidence:** raw_trips max_date=2016-03-10 vs mart_daily_summary max_date=2016-03-01

**Recommended action:** Resolve upstream staging blockage first; implement mart pipeline monitoring to detect future halts.

### 4. [WARNING] data_gap — `raw_trips`

Massive 335-day data gap indicates severe source ingestion issues or missing historical data.

**Evidence:** Gap from 2015-01-31 to 2016-01-01; 9 additional gaps totaling 380+ missing days

**Recommended action:** Validate source data completeness; audit ingestion scheduler and error logs for historical loads.

### 5. [WARNING] empty_load — `raw_trips`

Repeated near-empty loads suggest source system anomalies or ingestion failures on specific dates.

**Evidence:** 2016-01-03: 4 rows, 2016-02-25: 2 rows (both flagged as anomalies)

**Recommended action:** Verify source data availability on low-volume dates; implement threshold alerts for abnormal row counts.

### 6. [WARNING] empty_load — `staging_trips`

Propagated near-empty loads from upstream raw_trips, with additional row reduction indicating transformation risks.

**Evidence:** 2016-01-03: 3 rows (vs raw 4), 2016-02-25: 2 rows (vs raw 2)

**Recommended action:** Review transformation logic on low-volume dates; ensure no accidental filtering of small batches.
